Add BrightMind to your home screen

BrightMind is a web app — no app store needed. Install it from your browser and it opens full-screen, just like a native app.

iPhone & iPad
  1. Open brightmind in your browser.
  2. Tap the Share button ↑ in the toolbar.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add — the BrightMind icon appears on your home screen.
Android
  1. Open brightmind in your browser.
  2. Open the browser menu ⋮ (usually top-right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  4. Confirm with Add / Install.

Any browser works. On a desktop browser, look for the install icon in the address bar.
